Birthday flowers

Malti Flowers exists to simplify sending beautiful, unique flower arrangements from a local florist across Canada. Simply enter your flower delivery postcode in the box at the top of the page to view all birthday flower bouquets by independent florists for delivery.

Anniversary flowers

Every wedding anniversary is a celebration of another year spent together as a married couple, and the perfect occasion to gift your better half something truly memorable - like flowers from a local independent florist. Our partnering florists have curated a unique selection of anniversary flowers, particularly for milestone wedding anniversaries (congratulations to you if you've got one coming!), so select an anniversary bouquet from Malti in confidence.

Wedding flowers

You've got the venue, the dress, the guest list, the food, the vows, the ring (hopefully)... now just the flowers remain for your big day. Get in touch with the Malti team to ensure that your floral vision can be realised, as we will connect you to the local florist in Canada best suited to your needs and location.
